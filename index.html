SHORT BIOGRAPHY Dr. Naigao Jin is an Assistant Professor with the School of Software at Dalian University of Technology. He received his Ph.D. degree in Signal Processing from Dalian University of Technology in 2008 under Prof. Fuliang Yin. As a visiting Scholar hosted by Prof. Tian He at UMN from Jan. 2012 to Jan.2013. Naigao obtained B.E. and M.E. degrees from Dalian Jiaotong University and Dalian University of Technology in 1999 and 2002, respectively. Naigao is currently a member of IEEE Computer Society.
RESEARCH INTERESTS I enjoy building embedded systems and have a strong passion for computer systems, wireless networking, mobile and energy-aware computing, event-driven OS, radio technology, digital signal processing and robotics that enable new commercial applications and explore potential research directions. Currently, my research theme is Networked Embedded Computing Systems (NECS) including but not limited to the following topics
– Wireless Networking, Embedded and Mobile Computing.
– Localization and Tracking Systems, Network Time Synchronization.
– Energy-aware Computing, Event-driven Real-time OS, and Embedded Security.
